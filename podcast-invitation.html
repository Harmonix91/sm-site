What This Is

A conversation series with the executives who manage portfolios of physical assets — energy, infrastructure, facilities, industrial — and drive value creation from them.

We're talking to Portfolio Directors, Heads of Asset Management, and the platform leaders they work with. Not about theory. About what actually works. What it takes to align your work to value creation. How you earn the economics that reflect your contribution. The conversations that matter but rarely happen in public.

This is for operators, by an operator.

Why This Exists

Nobody talks openly about how asset management leaders prove their value and get compensated for it. The secrecy helps the people with leverage. It hurts the people trying to figure out what's normal to ask for.

This podcast breaks that silence. Real conversations with leaders who've earned meaningful equity, carry, or performance-based compensation — asking what they actually did to get there, how the conversation happened, and what they'd tell someone in their position now.

The goal: help the next generation of asset management leaders understand what it takes. Not guesswork. Lived experience.

Who We're Looking For

Portfolio Directors and Heads of Asset Management who:

  • Manage portfolios of revenue-generating physical assets (energy, infrastructure, facilities, industrial)
  • Have earned meaningful economics (equity, carry, performance comp tied to value creation)
  • Are willing to share honestly about their journey — the negotiations, the turning points, what worked and what didn't
  • Want to help others navigate the same path

Also interested in:

  • Platform CEOs/CFOs/COOs who work closely with Portfolio Directors and can speak to the relationship
  • Fund-side executives who've built or managed asset management functions

Post-exit or established leaders preferred — you're at a point where you can be candid in ways people still building their careers can't be.

The Conversation

Each episode is 45-60 minutes, edited to 30-40 minutes of the most valuable content.

Topics we cover:

  • Your journey into asset management / portfolio leadership
  • When you realized your compensation should be tied to value creation, not just performance
  • How you aligned your work to drive value (not just keep assets running)
  • The conversation with your board/GP about equity, carry, or performance comp — what you said, how they responded
  • What you got, and what you'd do differently knowing what you know now
  • What you'd tell someone in your position today

What you control:

  • Questions sent beforehand (no surprises)
  • Final edit review before it goes live
  • How specific you get about numbers (ranges work fine)
  • Whether you want to name companies/funds or keep it principles-based

This isn't gotcha journalism. It's creating the resource we all wish existed when we were starting out.

Why Participate

Your story helps people. The operators who listen aren't thinking you're bragging — they're thinking "how do I do that?" You're teaching, not showing off.

You're breaking new ground. Nobody else is having these conversations publicly. The first leaders willing to share set the tone for everyone who follows.

You join a community. Other guests, other leaders in asset management who are all trying to figure out the same things. This becomes a network, not just a podcast.

You control the narrative. This is you telling your story on your terms. Questions upfront, edit review, your choice on specifics.
