Beyond the Model. Where Value is Created.

Investors used to buy infrastructure for stable yield and leave it alone. Not any more. Across infrastructure, industrial, and the services platforms underneath them, value creation is the whole game — and it isn't delivered by the model. It's delivered, quarter after quarter, by the fund-side Asset Managers, Operating Partners, and Platform CEOs who turn underwriting into operating reality.

This is where they tell us how. Not theory. Real stories from the people in the room.

Who We're Looking For

Three voices, at the centre of every value creation story in PE-backed real assets. Fund-side Asset Managers, overseeing portfolio performance and managing the relationship with portco leadership. Operating Partners with the mandate to drive change across portfolios and shape the value creation playbook. Platform CEOs executing it on the ground — ideally post-exit, so they can talk openly.

People willing to share the real stories. What the model assumed, what the operators had to do, what worked, what didn't.

The Format

A 45–60 minute conversation, in person or over a video call. Edited down to 30–40 minutes for release.

After a quick intro on the platform and your role, the arc moves through six areas:

  1. The investment thesis — the underlying economics and the value creation plan
  2. The risks — what could derail the thesis, what the team is watching
  3. What the model doesn't capture — operating reality vs. the underwriting
  4. Curveballs and wins — the moves that actually drove value
  5. Aligning to the plan — how you set priorities from your seat
  6. Building influence — across the sponsor, the board, and the operating team that shapes every decision

You control the detail level. Questions sent ahead. Edit review before it goes live.
